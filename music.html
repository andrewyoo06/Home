BTS is a name that every one knows, whether its because they are a fan or simple because everyone keeps on talking about them. They are the biggest boy group in the world! They were orignally supposed to debut as a hip-hop group and have showed other musical sides to them as the years have gone by. They consist of 4 vocalists and 3 rappers, which are all incredibly talented. However in my opinion, their english releases, "Dynamite" and "Butter", are just not as good as their previous releases. These are "ok" songs but it does not show how skilled BTS is in terms of dancing, rapping or singing. Sadly these are their most popular songs and many people do not realize the other musical side of BTS. So today I will not be showing you those songs,I will be showing you these 3 diffrent songs. They will each show-case a diffrent side of BTS. For the first song, it is called "Mic Drop", by BTS. This song calls out the people who didn't beleive in them or simply are "hating" on them. Also, if you want to see more songs that are just 100% rap, check out the Cypher series or the solo works of RM, Suga, and J-Hope. Next, is a song called "ON". This is one of the most advanced songs in terms of dancing. There is so much complex forms of dancing and they have filmed and sang this song in many places, but in my opinion their preformance at the Grand Central Station in New York was their best. Lastly, is my favourite BTS song, called "Life Goes On". This song really struck a chord with me as this was realised in quarantine and the lyrics describe the endless waiting, and how life still goes on. All of their songs are good and BTS has something for everyone, whether it be ballads or rap!
